Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the conference ticket?

Your ticket includes full access to all keynote sessions, breakout tracks, networking lounges, and complimentary lunch and coffee throughout the 3-day event. Pro and Team passes include additional exclusive perks.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes! All main stage talks and track sessions will be recorded. Standard pass holders get access after 30 days, while Pro and Team pass holders get raw, unedited access within 24 hours.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

We offer full refunds up to 30 days before the event. Within 30 days, you can easily transfer your ticket to a colleague or friend at no extra cost. Please contact support for assistance.

Do I need to bring my own laptop?

While you can enjoy the keynote and panel discussions without one, bringing your own laptop is highly recommended if you plan to participate in the hands-on coding workshops and hackathons.

Is there a discount for students?

Absolutely! We believe in empowering the next generation of developers. We offer a 50% discount on Standard passes for currently enrolled university students. Bring your valid Student ID to the venue.

Will food and drinks be provided?

Yes, catered lunch and unlimited premium coffee, tea, and snacks will be available throughout all three days for all attendees. VIP ticket holders will also get access to the exclusive networking dinner.

Is there a Code of Conduct?

Yes. DevConf is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, or religion. All attendees must abide by our Code of Conduct.
